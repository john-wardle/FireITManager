# Requirements

## Incident Management
- Create and manage incident records
- Link incidents to camps, assets, and personnel
- Track incident status and operational context

## Inventory
- Track equipment, supplies, and consumables
- Record ownership and location
- Support filtering and reporting of inventory state

## Networking
- Model incident network topology
- Represent devices, cables, and their relationships
- Support future visualization and troubleshooting workflows

## Reports
- Produce incident summaries and operational reports
- Generate inventory and network reports
- Support export of structured data

## GIS
- Support geographic or site-based planning concepts
- Associate assets and infrastructure with incident locations

## Help Desk
- Track support issues and operational tickets
- Link support cases to incidents and relevant assets

## Administration
- Manage configuration and application settings
- Support role-based maintenance workflows

## Non-Functional Requirements
- The application should be maintainable and modular.
- It should support future extension without major rewrites.
- It should provide a responsive desktop experience.
- It should be suitable for field and office environments.
